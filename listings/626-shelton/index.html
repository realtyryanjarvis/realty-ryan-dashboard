The Residence

626 Shelton Street in Charlotte's Mammoth Oaks offers a fresh take on refined living. This new construction delivers smart design and practical luxury. Enter through a sleek foyer or a convenient friends' entry. The kitchen, with a scullery, streamlines meal prep and hosting. Outside, a pool and covered porch create an inviting space for relaxation or gatherings. Inside, a loft adds flexible living space, while the bonus room features a built-in homework area and bar for work or play. A utility drop zone keeps things tidy, and a three-car garage offers ample storage. Located in tranquil Mammoth Oaks, you're minutes from SouthPark's dining and shopping. 626 Shelton Street blends function, style, and prime location.
