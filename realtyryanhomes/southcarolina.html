SC vs NC

The Tax Advantage

South Carolina's tax structure offers real savings for homeowners. Here's how the SC side stacks up against North Carolina.

4%

Owner-Occupied Rate

SC taxes primary residences at just 4% of assessed value ‚Äî compared to NC's 100% valuation method. This results in meaningfully lower annual property tax bills.

$50K

Homestead Exemption

SC homeowners 65+ (or disabled) can exempt the first $50,000 of fair market value from property taxes ‚Äî a significant benefit for retirees.

0%

Tax on Groceries

South Carolina charges no state sales tax on groceries. Combined with generally lower property taxes, the day-to-day cost of living tilts in SC's favor.
